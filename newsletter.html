Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I receive the newsletter? +
Our main newsletter is sent weekly every Friday. Occasionally, we send special edition newsletters for major book releases or literary events.
Is the newsletter really free? +
Yes! ReadMedia Weekly is completely free. We're passionate about sharing great literature and building a community of readers.
Can I unsubscribe at any time? +
Absolutely. Every newsletter includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can also email us directly to be removed from the list.
What type of content can I expect? +
Each issue includes: 2-3 book recommendations with detailed reviews, author interviews or features, literary analysis of classics, reading challenges, and occasional exclusive content from our team.
Do you share my email with third parties? +
Never. We take your privacy seriously. Your email is used exclusively for sending ReadMedia newsletters and will never be sold or shared with third parties.
