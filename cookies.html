1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ReadMedia ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How Cookies Work

When you visit our website, we may place cookies on your device. These cookies may be:

  • First-party cookies: Set by us directly
  • Third-party cookies: Set by our partners and service providers
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until deleted
