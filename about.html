Our Journey

From a simple idea to a growing platform for readers worldwide

2024

The Beginning

ReadMedia was founded by a team of passionate readers and technologists who saw the need for a centralized platform for quality literary content. We started with 50 curated books and basic review functionality.

2024

Platform Growth

We expanded our library to 500+ books, introduced AI-powered recommendations, and built a community of 10,000+ monthly readers. Our team grew to include professional reviewers and literary experts.

Present

Global Reach

Today, ReadMedia serves readers in 25+ countries, with content available in multiple formats. We've helped thousands discover new favorite books and authors while maintaining our commitment to accessibility.

Future

Our Vision

We're working on expanding to audiobooks, building reading communities, and developing tools to help educators and book clubs. Our goal is to become the most trusted literary resource for readers worldwide.
